About the Project

Our concept aims to replace traditional **Silicon** material in AI chips with the new **Perovskite** material, which offers higher electrical efficiency, lower power consumption, and is more environmentally friendly. The design features an **advanced encapsulation system** to protect the sensitive Perovskite core.

Using Google AI tools like **Vertex AI**, we simulate the performance of this new material and predict its behavior compared to Silicon in terms of thermal efficiency and energy consumption.

🔬 Perovskite Chip Layers (Encapsulation)

The Encapsulated Perovskite structure consists of four protective layers around the core: 1. **Inner Insulator** (for thermal isolation, non-electrically conductive, but permeable to signals to maintain chip function), 2. **Cellulose Layer** (sandwich structure for mechanical stability), 3. **PVA Layer** (adhesive layer), and 4. **Outer Protection Layer** (moisture barrier and stabilization for all underlying layers).

🔄 Vertex AI Prediction

Based on Vertex AI simulation, the Encapsulated Perovskite chip will provide 80% power savings and 46% higher thermal efficiency compared to Silicon, along with dramatically improved long-term stability.
